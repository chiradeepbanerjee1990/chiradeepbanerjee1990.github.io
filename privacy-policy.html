Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2024

The short version: Fruit Rain Kids does not ask children to create an account or provide their name, email address, phone number or precise location.

LittleJoy Games ("we", "us" or "our") operates the Fruit Rain Kids mobile application (the "Service"). This policy explains how information is handled when the Service is used.

1. Overview and child safety

Fruit Rain Kids is designed for toddlers and preschoolers. We take children's privacy seriously and configure the Service to comply with applicable child-safety requirements, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play Families policies.

2. Personal information

We do not ask users for personally identifying information. In particular:

  • No name or email address is requested in the app.
  • No account, signup or login is required.
  • The app does not access contacts or phone numbers.
  • The app does not request precise GPS location.

3. Analytics and diagnostics

We use Google Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand gameplay and improve stability. These services may process non-personal usage and technical information such as screens viewed, levels started or completed, difficulty, time spent, scores, device type, operating-system version and crash diagnostics.

4. Advertising

The app displays banner advertising provided by Google AdMob. AdMob is configured for child-directed treatment. Personalised advertising and remarketing are disabled, and advertising is filtered for age-appropriate content.

5. Service providers

Google provides Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics and AdMob. These providers process information only to deliver their respective analytics, stability and advertising services, subject to their own terms and privacy practices.

6. Payments

If the app offers a premium unlock, the transaction is handled by Google Play. We do not collect or store payment-card or banking information.

7. Data security

Information transmitted to Firebase and AdMob is encrypted in transit using HTTPS. No method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, but we use reasonable measures to protect information handled by the Service.
